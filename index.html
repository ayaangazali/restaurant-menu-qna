About Us

The Copper Spoon is a family run restaurant in the heart of downtown. We have been serving up home style cooking since 2011. Everything on our menu is made from scratch using local ingredients whenever we can get them. Stop by for lunch, dinner, or just a coffee and a slice of pie.
